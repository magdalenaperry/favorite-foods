Artisan Pizza Dough

  • 500 g all-purpose flour
  • 275 g water
  • 5 g kosher salt
  • 2.5 g active-dry yeast
  1. mix flour and salt in mixing bowl
  2. warm 5 g of water and mix the yeast and ferment for approximately 5 minutes
  3. mix remaining water and flour and mix until forming a shaggy dough
  4. let dough rest for 30 minutes
  5. add the yeast and water mixture to shaggy dough and lightly pinch into dough to incorporate
